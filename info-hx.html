Philosophia ● Haxor ● praxis
Jugaad is a colloquial term in Hindi that refers to informal solutions for everyday situations or problems. Its closest English equivalent would be 'kludge' or 'hack.' There have been too many situations and problems throughout my life that have required informal solutions. I have become adept in hacking my way through jobs, school, and social connections. "if you want to get things done, you have to learn to reconnoiter your way around poorly designed and executed processes and systems" (Philip, Irani, Dourish 2010).
